Claude Monet Life

Picasso life

Picasso was a famous spanish painter.Pablo Picasso was born in October 25, 1881, in Malaga. His original name was Pablo Ruiz, He later changed his name to his mother's maiden name Pablo Picasso. Picasso’s Mother was Doña Maria Picasso y Lopez, his father was Don José Ruiz Blasco. His father was an art teacher and painter. As young child Picasso had immense talent for art. Due to this his father began teaching him, as he grew up he spent more time drawing rather than doing school work.

1895, Picasso and his family moved to Barcelona, Spain. He later was accepted to one of the best art schools in the area. He moved to Madrid where the school was located.Due to not licking the strict rules that were placed he began skipping classes.

Once he turned 16 in 1897 he moved back to Barcelona. There he met with other artists in the cafe “ El Quatre Gats “. There he became inspired to attempt different art styles.

Later he moved to Paris, This is where he experienced his blue period. This a period where picasso went through a stage of depression. The period was named this due to the blue that dominated most of his paintings. This period lasted from 1901-1904. In this period he painted scenes of poverty, pain, and isolation.

Later during the start of world war one picasso began again to get into realism.Between 1918-1927 became known as his classic period. Then began his break into surrealism. Surrealism came from picasso's cubism. One of his most well known painting was a surrealist due to a spanish bombing that had occurred. The painting being “guernica”. It still is a powerful anti war painting used to this day.

Picasso continue to create art till his late years. He died on April 8th,1973 at the age of 91. He died in Morgins, France. His art and legacy still live on though and he is still one of the most well known painters to this day.
